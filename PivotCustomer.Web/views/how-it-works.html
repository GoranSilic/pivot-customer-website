Before you quit, Pivot.

Pivot starts out easy - with a nine-day program that helps you understand your smoking behavior. Because once you know where you are, it's easier to figure out how to get where you want to go.

Pivot

How it works

Pivot has four main elements designed to work together for a truly unique and personalized experience. Before you start, you'll learn to get the most out of each one.

The Pivot Journey

The Pivot Journey unfolds over six stages, each based on clinical guidelines and behavioral science.

stage one:

Explore

It's true: We don't start with quit. We start with discovery. During these nine days, your employees get to know their coach, use their new breath sensor, and complete daily activities.

Goal: Increased awareness of smoking behavior and how it affects personal and professional lives

9

stage two:

Build

Participants who are ready to quit work with their coach to build a quit plan in as quickly as one day. Those who aren’t continue with activities and coaching designed to develop skills and confidence to quit.

Goal: Increased readiness to quit

1-4

stage three:

Mobilize

Your employees execute their quit plan, based on evidence-based strategies shown to increase the likelihood of success—such as acquiring medications, arranging support, and preparing a craving kit.

Goal: Commitment to quit and increased likelihood of success

1

stage four:

Quit

Because Pivot approaches quitting as a skillset, rather than a single act, your employees now have the skills they need to take this next step.

Goal: A successful quit attempt

1

stage five:

Secure

And because you’ve provided a platform that is with them for the long haul, your newly smoke-free employees receive continued coaching, lessons, and practice designed for the vital first few weeks after a quit attempt.

Goal: Coping with the challenges that come immediately after a quit

11

stage six:

Sustain

The focus shifts to maintenance. For up to a full year after their quit date, your employees continue to build skills and confidence, while their coaches provide support designed to prevent relapse.

Goal: A smoke-free life

40
