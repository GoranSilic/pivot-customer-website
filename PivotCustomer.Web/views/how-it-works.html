Before you quit, Pivot.

Pivot starts out easy - with a nine-day program that helps you understand your smoking behavior. Because once you know where you are, it's easier to figure out how to get where you want to go.

Pivot

How it works

Pivot has four main elements designed to work together for a truly unique and personalized experience. Before you start, you'll learn to get the most out of each one.

Sensing
You'll use the Pivot Breath Sensor to measure carbon monoxide (CO) in your breath. CO is one of the toxins that enter your bloodstream when you smoke. Measuring it throughout the day will give you a view of your exposure to cigarette smoke.
Logging
The Pivot App's simple logging tool will let you track the cigarettes you smoke so you can see things like how much time you spend smoking and how your smoking patterns affect your CO levels.
Learning
Pivot's interactive lessons and activities will help you discover new things about your smoking, and they can walk you through proven techniques that break the challenge of quitting into small, manageable steps.
Coaching
You're not in this alone. Pivot's trained coaches will work with you one-on-one to help you understand your smoking behaviors and decide whether or not you're interested in changing them.

The Pivot Journey

The full Pivot experience unfolds over six stages designed to support you for up to 13 months. Each stage uses behavioral science to help you set and reach personal goals.

stage one:

Explore

It’s true: We don’t start with quit. We start with discovery. During these nine days, you get to know your coach, use your new breath sensor, and complete daily activities.

Goal: To become more aware of your smoking behavior

9

stage two:

Build

If you’re ready, you can work with your coach to build a quit plan in as quickly as one day. If you’re still not sure, you can continue with activities and coaching designed to develop skills and confidence.

Goal: Building skills and confidence

1-4

stage three:

Mobilize

Now that you’ve built a quit plan, you’ll work with your coach to set it in motion, using proven strategies like choosing quit medications, arranging support, and preparing a craving kit.

Goal: Setting yourself up for a successful quit

1

stage four:

Quit

Because Pivot approaches quitting as a skillset, rather than a single act, you now have the skills they need to take this next step—with the full support of your coach and the plan you laid out.

Goal: A full week without smoking

1

stage five:

Secure

Pivot is with you for the long haul. You’ll receive continued coaching, lessons, and practice designed for the important first few weeks after you quit.

Goal: Adjusting to, and embracing, your new smoke-free life

11

stage six:

Sustain

For up to a full year after your quit date, Pivot will help you continue to build skills and confidence, while your coaches continue to deliver support and guidance

Goal: Locking in change for good

40
