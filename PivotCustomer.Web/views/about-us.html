About Us
Pivot was created by Carrot Sense, a digital health company that's using behavioral science, mobile technology, and clinical expertise to engage and empower millions of people to quit smoking.
A note from our founder, David S. Utley

"We believe in smokers." This is one of our value statements at Carrot. It affirms our belief that smokers are human beings with real-life daily routines, stresses, family lives, work lives, fun times, feelings, goals, and aspirations. We believe that smokers are capable of successfully quitting if we support them with powerful tools, awesome coaching, and proven quit strategies. By believing in smokers, we have designed our product offering with a positive supportive voice. We live this value statement everyday.

During one of our user-feedback sessions, a woman who had participated in Pivot saw “We believe in smokers” on the wall and said, “Wow. I’ve never heard anyone say that to me or anyone else before!” I was surprised, as I hadn’t realized that a core value statement, intended to be an internal belief and guideline for our company, would resonate so strongly with our users! But that’s how it should be, right—having your internal and external beliefs and priorities fully aligned?

Why would “Believing in smokers” be so surprising? I mean, we have all been touched by the adverse effects of smoking. Everyone has a family member who smoked or smokes. We all know friends and family who have suffered an illness or died from smoking. No one would intentionally not get behind someone to help them quit smoking, right?

I think what has happened at the public level is that we have become numb and desensitized to the fact that there is pervasive use of tobacco in our society. And we are numbed to how much damage tobacco use does to our environment, our people, our pets, our kids, our economy, our everything. Numb. It’s not that we have given up on smokers; we given up on trying to do something about smoking.

At Carrot, we refuse to give up. With Pivot, we’ve created an experience that people want to use. And we pack the experience with the best strategies, tools, and coaching. We don’t try to make people quit smoking. We want to empower and engage people so that they can quit on their own terms, for themselves.

Because we believe in smokers.
